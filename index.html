ABOUT FORGE

A floor built for
real training

FORGE started in 2016 as a single power rack in a converted warehouse. No juice bar, no towel service — just enough space and steel to let people get strong without waiting for equipment.

Today it's 12,000 square feet of platforms, sleds, and open floor, run by coaches who program your plan instead of just checking you in. Every membership includes a real assessment, not a form and a fingerprint scan.
